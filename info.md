# PlexCleanup

A single-file browser tool for cleaning up your Plex library.

Works with just Plex alone — Tautulli is optional but recommended for the best experience. Radarr and Sonarr are optional for full-stack deletion.

Open the HTML file in Safari or another modern browser, enter your Plex credentials, and you're ready. Add Tautulli to unlock the Half-watched filter. Add Radarr and/or Sonarr to route deletions through your arr stack.

No installation. No external services. No cloud.
